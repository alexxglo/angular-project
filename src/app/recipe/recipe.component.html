Chicken Noodles

Description

Chicken noodles is one of my favourite and go-to recipes when i have to prepare a quick meal. This one-pan dish is hassle free and extremely economical - great for students who hate doing the dishes.

Preparation time
30 minutes
Ingredients
  • Chicken breast
  • Chinese noodles
  • Soy sauce
    • Steps

    Apply soy sauce to the chicken breast.Leave to marinate for at least 8 hours.

    Place the marinated chicken for 35-40 minutes in the oven. Add water in a pot and wait for it to start boiling.

    After the water boils, pour the noodles into the pan and let it sit there for 5 minutes.

    Drain the noodles and place them into a frying pan alongside the chicken and pour some more soy sauce. Mix.

    Enjoy!
